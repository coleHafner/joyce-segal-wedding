Green Gates at Flowing Lake (View Site)

Green Gates is located on Flowing Lake near the historic town of Snohomish, Washington.

Directions
  • From Seattle, take I-5 North to exit 194. Take the ramp right for US-2 East toward
  • Wenatchee/Snohomish (24.3 mi)
  • Bear right onto US-2/Stevens Pass Hwy (4 mi)
  • Turn left onto Westwick Rd (6.2 mi)
  • Road changes to 171st
  • Turn right onto 48th
  • Turn right onto 175th
  • Destination is on the left. Look for the Joyce-Segal sign!
  • Arrive at 4919 175th
